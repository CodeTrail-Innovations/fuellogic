Fuelogic Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: July 12, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We only collect essential data to create and manage your account:

  • Name - To personalize your experience
  • Email address - For account communication
  • Role - To determine access privileges (company or driver)
  • Authentication credentials - Securely stored for login purposes

We do not collect or store unnecessary personal or sensitive information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for service delivery:

  • Account authentication and security
  • Service improvement and feature development
  • Essential notifications regarding your account
  • Basic analytics to enhance app performance

3. Data Sharing

We maintain strict confidentiality of your information:

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data
  • Limited sharing occurs only with essential service providers:
    • Push notification services (FCM)
    • Analytics providers (non-identifiable data only)
  • All partners adhere to strict data protection standards

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transfers
  • Secure Firebase storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Authentication protocols to prevent unauthorized access

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained only while your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • All personal data is permanently removed
  • Transactional records are anonymized
  • Deletion occurs within 30 days of account closure

6. International Compliance

Fuelogic adheres to global privacy regulations:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA/CPRA (California, USA)
  • PIPEDA (Canada)
  • LGPD (Brazil)
  • Other applicable privacy laws

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 13:

  • We do not knowingly collect data from children
  • Accounts must be created by individuals 13 years or older
  • Contact us immediately if you believe a child's data was collected

8. Your Rights

You maintain control over your data:

  • Access and review your stored information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights or for privacy-related inquiries:

Email us at: privacy@fuellogict.com

We respond to all requests within 30 days.

9. Policy Updates

We may periodically update this policy:

  • Changes will be communicated via email or app notification
  • Significant modifications will be highlighted
  • Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance

10. Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy:

Email: privacy@fuellogict.com

We value your privacy and will respond promptly to all inquiries.
